Community

Eating well is easier together. Alongside the app, Forkcast will run free, health-focused community events with our partner restaurants.

No events are scheduled yet. The calendar opens when Forkcast launches in Boston with its first verified partner restaurants. What follows is the plan — not a promise of dates.

What we're planning

Neighborhood walk & lunch

A guided group walk that ends at a verified partner restaurant, with a best-fit menu preview before you arrive.

Partner kitchen tastings

Independent restaurants walk through how a dish is made — portions, oils, swaps — and verify its nutrition with diners in the room.

Eating out with a condition

Practical sessions on navigating menus with hypertension, diabetes, or food allergies — with a dietitian, not influencer advice.

GLP-1 companions table

Small-group meetups for people on GLP-1 medications figuring out restaurant portions and protein together.

Run a restaurant?

Community events are built with partner restaurants — hosting a tasting is one of the ways diners meet you.
